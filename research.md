## üîç Market Research Overview

### Problem Validation

#### Current State of Offense Reporting in Malaysia & Singapore

**Malaysia:**
- **Royal Malaysia Police (PDRM)**: Online reporting through e-Reporting system
- **Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission (MACC)**: Separate portal for corruption reports
- **Road Transport Department (JPJ)**: Traffic violation reporting
- **Local Councils**: Municipal offense reporting systems

**Singapore:**
- **Singapore Police Force (SPF)**: i-Witness online reporting
- **Land Transport Authority (LTA)**: Traffic violation portal
- **National Environment Agency (NEA)**: Environmental offense reporting
- **Housing Development Board (HDB)**: Estate-related complaints

#### Pain Points Identified:
1. **Fragmented Systems**: Multiple portals for different offense types
2. **Complex Navigation**: Users struggle to find the right department
3. **No Cross-Border Integration**: Separate systems for each country
4. **Limited Feedback**: Minimal status updates on report progress
5. **Mobile Unfriendly**: Most systems not optimized for smartphones

### Direct Competitors

#### 1. Malaysia e-Reporting (PDRM)
- **Strengths**: Official government backing, established user base
- **Weaknesses**: Limited to police matters, poor mobile experience
- **Market Share**: ~60% of online police reports in Malaysia
- **User Rating**: 2.8/5 (Google Play Store)

#### 2. Singapore i-Witness (SPF)
- **Strengths**: Well-designed interface, government integration
- **Weaknesses**: Singapore-only, limited offense categories
- **Market Share**: ~70% of online police reports in Singapore
- **User Rating**: 3.5/5 (App Store)

#### 3. MyDistress (Malaysia)
- **Strengths**: Emergency focus, GPS integration
- **Weaknesses**: Emergency-only, limited reporting features
- **Market Share**: ~15% of emergency reporting apps
- **User Rating**: 4.1/5

### Indirect Competitors

#### 1. SeeClickFix (Global)
- **Strengths**: Community-driven, municipal integration
- **Weaknesses**: Not focused on law enforcement, US-centric
- **Market Share**: Used in 300+ cities globally

#### 2. Waze (Traffic Reporting)
- **Strengths**: Real-time traffic data, large user base
- **Weaknesses**: Limited to traffic, no official integration
- **Market Share**: 140+ million monthly active users

#### 3. Nextdoor (Community Reporting)
- **Strengths**: Neighborhood focus, social features
- **Weaknesses**: Social platform, not official reporting
- **Market Share**: 27+ million weekly active users
